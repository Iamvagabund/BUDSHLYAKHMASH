about us

Budshlyakhmash Group specializes on the production, supply and after-sales service of municipal and road-maintenance vehicles on the market of Ukraine for over 20 years. Management of JSC Budshlyakhmash is a team of highly skilled experts in spheres of engineering, production and management. Due to their efforts the company became one of the leaders on the Ukrainian market.

The company Budshlyakhmash was founded in 1993 in Kyiv, Ukraine. In 90-s it became a dealer for the Russian companies GAZ, Arzamas Engineering Plant Kommash, Mtsensk Engineering Plant - Kommash, KAMAZ, and huge Ukrainian trucks producer KRAZ.

In 2002 the company opened an Engineering Plant Spetsbudmash as well as service centres in the biggest cities of Ukraine.

Now, the company employs over 500 people, including 76 engineers and technicians. It has its own construction department that develops new models and improves old ones. These advantages make it possible for the plant to produce over 100 vehicles per month.

Budshlyakhmash Group offers a wide range of products (over 70) for municipal and road-maintenance needs. The company has individual and flexible approach to its clients. The equipment can be mounted on different chassis of MAZ, ZIL, GAZ, KAMAZ, KRAZ, Isuzu, and Tata. Any other kind of the chassis can be also mounted on request. The vehicles can be also customized for hot or cold climate.

Budshlyakhmash Group is proud to provide the products that are made according to the highest standards for competitive price on the markets of Europe and Asia. Now the company has an ambitious aim to work up the markets of Middle East and Africa.
