Road Combined Machine (or Multipurpose Road Maintenance Vehicle) MDK/MDKZ

These vehicles are used for year-round maintenance of hard surface roads. They can be used with road washing attachments to clean and water the road, roadsides and roadside plantation.

Special equipment includes a tank, a sand spreader, an angling blade, high-speed blades, a front brush, a central brush, and a brush for crash barriers cleaning.

Main advantages:

  • Ease of operation;
  • Broad choice of attachments;
  • Possibility to adopt the vehicles in accordance with the needs of the client;
  • Possibility to use different chassis, including ZIL, MAZ, KAMAZ, and KRAZ. As a result, tank capacity can vary from 6 up to 12 m3.
