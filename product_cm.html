Combined Machine

Combined machines (or multipurpose vehicles) KO-503IVK with the mounted sewer jetter and sewage suction equipment are used for preventive cleaning of wells and urban storm-water drainage system of sludge, soil sediments and other pollutants, as well as removing of dangerous blockages there. Sewer jetter equipment is used for cleaning of groundwater wells and septic tanks of industrial plants treatment facilities, as well as loading, transportation and unloading of nonaggressive and non-hazardous liquid waste. The vehicle can also be used for washing cars, equipment and various surfaces by high pressure jets. The main base chassis used for this vehicle is KRAZ, however other chassis can also be used.

Main advantages:

  • Ease of operation;
  • High quality of treatment;
  • Reliable pumps performance;
  • Possibility of operating in different weather conditions;
  • Possibility to adopt the vehicles in accordance with the needs of the client.
