Emergency Machine

Emergency vehicles are used for quick delivery of the emergency crew and necessary equipment to the accident site. Different kinds of vans can be produced and equipped upon request of the client.

The possible equipment includes:

  • a folding table,
  • a drying cupboards for clothes,
  • a toolbox,
  • a workbench,
  • workbench tools,
  • a round-point shovel,
  • a square-point shovel,
  • a crowbar,
  • a sledgehammer,
  • a vice,
  • a jack,
  • an angle grinder,
  • a road cutter,
  • a reciprocating saw,
  • a drill,
  • a reversing valve,
  • a switchboard,
  • a power station,
  • an electric polishing stone,
  • an electric hammer,
  • 12 V, 220 V or 380 V cable,
  • an electric welding machine,
  • a welding cable,
  • a welding coil,
  • a welding outfit,
  • an electric water pump,
  • a hose,
  • a burner,
  • an oxygen hose,
  • a propane hose,
  • a propane or oxygen cylinder,
  • an oxygen or propane cylinder mount,
  • an interior van heater,
  • a sticker,
  • a torch,
  • a searchlight,
  • a ladder,
  • a hand winch,
  • grounding pegs,
  • a protective fence,
  • a temporary triangular warning sign,
  • a fire extinguisher,
  • a first-aid kit.

Any additional equipment is available upon request.
