Machine for Pit Repair

Machines for pit repair (or pothole patchers) UYAR-01 are used for mechanical pothole patching of bituminous concrete surface of the roadways.

Special characteristics:

  • Air-powered supply of broken stones;
  • Rotary compressor Jurop DL-80 of positive displacement type;
  • Camozzi boost controller;
  • Emulsion temperature control and gas heating;
  • The tank with emulsion is insulated which decreases gas consumption for the heating;
  • Emulsion is supplied automatically, which can also be operated from the truck cab;
  • Airlift of working element (liftarm);
  • Two screw conveyors allow moving differently graded broken stones to the spray nozzle (a working element of the equipment);
  • The liftarm is mounted quite close to a surface (not more than 1,3 m), which increases its manoeuvrability, decreases the weight of liftarm and its wind resistance. The working radius of liftarm is 4,4 m;
  • Automatic mode of liftarm functioning which increases workforce productivity;
  • Circulation system prevents emulsion gelation at low temperatures;
  • The equipment has the pipes blow down and flushing system (to remove emulsion residues);
  • The hydraulic motor speed is controlled by the operator from the cabin, which makes it possible to change modes depending on the size of potholes.

Main advantages:

  • Maintenance can be made on wet surface;
  • No need for preliminary potholes treatment and cleaning;
  • No need for road rollers;
  • Decrease of labour input;
  • Materials saving;
  • Minimized traffic flow disturbances;
  • Possibility to use different chassis, including MAZ, KAMAZ, and KRAZ, as well as a trailer bed.
