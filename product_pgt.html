Portal Garbage Truck

Portal garbage trucks are used for collection and disposal of oversize urban and construction wastes, as well as for transportation and dump unloading of different bulk and oversize loads.

Special equipment includes a body, a frame, a portal, outriggers, locks, as well as hydraulic, pneumatic and electric systems.

The body can be dismounted off the frame using portal and spotted for loading on a flat area. The loading can be made manually or using any kinds of mechanical loaders.

Main advantages:

  • Capacious open detachable container;
  • Collection and transportation of any kind of waste (solid domestic, bulk and long-length construction waste);
  • Unloading by tipping;
  • Setting and fixing of a container at any intermediate position;
  • Safety during upward and downward container moving;
  • Use of one truck for several demountable containers if necessary for the huge territory;
  • Possibility to adopt the vehicles in accordance with the needs of the client;
  • Possibility to use different chassis, including ZIL, MAZ, KAMAZ, and GAZ. As a result, container capacity can vary from 5 up to 8 m3.
