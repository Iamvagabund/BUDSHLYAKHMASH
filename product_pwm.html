Pipe Washing Machine

Pipe washing machines (or sewer jetter trucks) KO-503KP are used for emergency and preventive sediments and blockages removal out of waste and rainwater pipes, sediment ponds, wells, storm-water drainage systems and sewerage systems. The special equipment includes a tank, an engine-driven water pump, a hose, a hydraulic system, and auxiliary equipment. The trucks are equipped with the set of sewer jetting nozzles which can remove sediments by high-pressure water jets. The types of nozzles used depend on the diameter of the pipes and contamination level. Auxiliary equipment includes washing equipment for high-pressure hose, an independent diesel air heater, a pump feeding system with antifreeze, a handgun with hose, set of sewer jetting nozzles, a filling hose, working areas light.

Main advantages:

  • Ease of operating;
  • High quality of treatment;
  • Reliable high-pressure pumps performance;
  • Possibility of operating in different weather conditions;
  • Flexible configurations and possibility of customization according to the needs of the client;
  • Possibility to use different chassis, including ISUZU, MAZ, KAMAZ, ZIL and GAZ. As a result, container capacity can vary from 3,5 up to 10 m3.
