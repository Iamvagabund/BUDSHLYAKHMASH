Rear Loading Garbage Truck

Rear loading garbage trucks are used for automated and manual solid urban waste collection, its compressing, transportation and automated unloading at disposal sites.

Special equipment includes a tailgate, a pushing plate, a loading-unloading device, hydraulic and electrical systems.

Wastes are collected manually when the hopper of the truck is lifted downwards. The waste is compacted by a pushing plate that scoops the waste out of the loading hopper and compresses it against a moving wall. To dump its load, pushing plate can be used or the body of the truck can be tilted, allowing the garbage to slide off it.

Main advantages:

  • Ease of operation;
  • Reliable performance;
  • Body and back header are made of high-strength steel 09G2S;
  • Scoop and lifting mechanisms are made of Swedish steel XARDOX400;
  • Convenient and quick garbage unload from plastic containers (DIN standard);
  • Tipper is operated manually, automatically and semi-automatically;
  • The location of vehicle control panel guarantees operator’s safety;
  • Containers are unloaded simultaneously with waste pressing;
  • Waste pressing guarantees high degree of garbage compaction;
  • Spillage of waste is minimized when loading the truck, making a vehicle environment-friendly;
  • Reliable hydraulics and electronics ensure minimum spending for maintenance and after-sales service;
  • Possibility to adopt the vehicles in accordance with the needs of the client;
  • Possibility to use different chassis, including MAZ, KAMAZ, and GAZ. As a result, container capacity can vary from 7,5 up to 20 m3, hopper capacity ranges from 0,6 up to 3 m3, weight of wastes can be as 3,1 tons as 11,5 tons.
