Sludge Pumping Machine

Sludge pumping machines (or sewage suction trucks) KO-503IV are used for preventive sanitary load of sludge, soil sediments and other pollutants out of groundwater wells, storm water sewers and drainage systems and their transportation to the place of discharge.
Special equipment includes a tank, an engine-driven vacuum pump, a suction boom, hydraulic, pneumatic and electric systems and auxiliary equipment. The suction boom is controlled by the remote control panel. To dump sludge, the body of the truck is tilted.

Main advantages:

  • Ease of operating;
  • High quality of treatment;
  • Reliable high-pressure pumps performance;
  • Possibility of operating in different weather conditions;
  • Flexible configurations and possibility of customization according to the needs of the client;
  • Possibility to use different chassis, including ISUZU, MAZ, KAMAZ, ZIL and KRAZ. As a result, container capacity can vary from 3,5 up to 10 m3.
