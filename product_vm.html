Vacuum Machine

Vacuum trucks KO-503V are used to pneumatically load liquid wastes from cesspits and wells, as well as transport and unload them to the place of liquid waste utilization that do not contain explosive and flammable substances.
The equipment includes a tank, an engine-driven vacuum pump, a four-way valve pipe, a safety alarm device, an air cock with suction boom, and electric systems. The tank is filled pneumatically, which is powered by vacuum pump. To empty the tank, vacuum pump can be used or the body of the truck can be tilted. For better equipment maintenance it is possible to produce a truck with fully opening rear door.

Main advantages:

  • Ease of operating;
  • High quality of treatment;
  • Reliable high-pressure pumps performance;
  • Possibility of operating in different weather conditions;
  • Flexible configurations and possibility of customization according to the needs of the client.
  • Possibility to use different chassis, including ISUZU, GAZ, MAZ, KAMAZ, ZIL and KRAZ. As a result, container capacity can vary from 3,75 up to 12 m3.
