Worldwide presence

Budshlyakhmash Group actively exports its products to the markets of Russia, Moldova, Belorussia, Kazakhstan, and Kyrgyzstan. It also cooperates with the partners from Armenia, Georgia, Uzbekistan, Tadzhikistan, Turkmenistan, Azerbaidžan.

Besides these European and Asian countries the company in cooperation with KRAZ exported its vehicles to Vietnam, Iraq, Cuba, and Afghanistan.

Budshlyakhmash is especially proud of the projects that were made for the EU market. We exported our combined machines (multipurpose vehicles) to Romania and Lithuania and vacuum trucks to Bulgaria.
